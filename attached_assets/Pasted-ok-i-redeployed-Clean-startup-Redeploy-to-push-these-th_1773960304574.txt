textGET https://bridge-relay.replit.app/api/grok-proxy?key=BRIDGE_KEY&project=landing-page&payload=BASE64
Where BASE64 is btoa('{"actions":[{"type":"screenshot_preview","project":"landing-page"}]}').
The bridge relay receives this GET, forwards the action over WebSocket to this Replit dev server, where sandbox-dispatcher.cjs launches Puppeteer, takes a PNG screenshot, uploads it to Catbox.moe, and returns the URL back through the WebSocket → relay → your executor.
This takes ~15-20 seconds.
Step 4 — You feed the result back to Grok:
You send another POST to /v1/responses with previous_response_id from step 1 and a function_call_output containing {"screenshotUrl":"https://files.catbox.moe/xyz.png"}.
Step 5 — Grok responds with the URL in plain English.
